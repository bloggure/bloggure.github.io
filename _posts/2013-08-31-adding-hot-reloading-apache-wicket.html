Recently, I did a small experiment for Apache Wicket : adding auto compile and hot reloading of changes.
In this short blog post I will try to describe how I added it. First of all, the basics of auto compile and hot reloading is heavily inspired by the work made by Xavier Hanin for Rest.x (this is a great framework I urge you to try if you've not already did).

Integrating Auto-compile

I've started from the sources of Rest.x and picked up the classes related to auto compile when Xavier implemented it. I made a copy/paste of the files to allow its modification without needing any Rest.x dependency.
